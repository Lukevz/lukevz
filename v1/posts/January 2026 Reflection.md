## ﻿1 lesson you learned last month
I learned that work is heavily an emotion-free space. There may be moments of levity – a quick joke, small talk before a meeting starts – but most people are heavily focused on “getting in and getting out.” I dealt with that at PwC quite a bit, but the work was very fulfilling or at least it was a rewarding space to be in. Instinct is very different and I’ve learned that specifically for me, with delayed AuDHD emotional processing, it will drain me over time if I’m not careful. How I show up to meetings, mask, or volunteer for work can put me in a position where I’m not always regulated or feel forced to react even if that pressure isn’t directly there. If I don’t agree with something, I can’t always explain why, it sometimes takes hours or even days to understand.
