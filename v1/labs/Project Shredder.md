# Project Shredder

A playful space-themed file shredder. Select or drag files to launch them into the black hole. Watch as your files are stowed in a pixel-art space capsule, launched into orbit, and gradually pulled into the event horizon where they disappear forever into the void.

No files are actually uploaded or stored - this is purely for the satisfaction of dramatically yeeting your unwanted files into oblivion.
