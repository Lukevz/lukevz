# Lost in helixes
Choice is born in the divergence of “do it because it is right” and “want to because it could be wrong”. It is when you can find the balance between your healthy risk tolerance and trying new things and doing what is conventional on paper wisdom that you can achieve good harmony. It’s all about [[emotional velocity]] which is often tied to financial stability and the more risk you can afford the more comfortable you can become. Money does not buy happiness, money takes enough hits for you to take a risks that the discovery of happiness might not always be successful.
