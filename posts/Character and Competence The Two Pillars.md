# Character and Competence: The Two Pillars
Trust is forged through a blend of **character** (who you are) and **competence** (what you can do). Character reflects integrity and intent, while competence shows up in capabilities and results. A deficit in either weakens trust. This framework is simple but powerful—it clarifies why good intentions alone aren’t enough.
